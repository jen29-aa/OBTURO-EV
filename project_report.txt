ABSTRACT
The Obturo-EV Intelligent Electric Vehicle (EV) Charger Booking and Management System is an
innovative web-based platform designed to revolutionize the electric vehicle charging experience
through intelligent decision-making and real-time availability integration. The system addresses
major challenges faced by EV users such as limited charger accessibility, booking conflicts, and
unreliable status updates. Obturo-EV enables users to search, compare, and reserve charging
stations based on personalized preferences including price, distance, waiting time, and charging
speed. By leveraging Multi-Attribute Decision-Making (MADM) methods and the TOPSIS algo’┐Šrithm, the system provides ranked recommendations that dynamically adapt to user priorities. It
further incorporates route-based discovery, conflict-free booking, and queue management to en’┐Šsure guaranteed charger availability. The inclusion of Peer-to-Peer (P2P) charger listings enhances
network scalability and optimizes infrastructure utilization. Built with robust authentication, pol’┐Šicy enforcement, and real-time data handling, Obturo-EV ensures fairness, reliability, and data
integrity throughout the charging process. Ultimately, this intelligent platform aims to promote
sustainable transportation by offering a unified, efficient, and user-centric EV charging experi’┐Šence.

INTRODUCTION
In todayŌĆÖs rapidly advancing world, the rise of electric vehicles (EVs) represents a major step
toward sustainable and eco-friendly transportation . However, despite the growing adoption of
EVs, one of the major challenges faced by users is the limited availability and accessibility of
charging infrastructure . Traditional EV charging systems rely mainly on static filtering methods,
such as proximity or price, which fail to address dynamic user needs like waiting time, budget, or
charging speed .
To overcome these challenges, the Obturo-EV Intelligent Charger Booking and Management Sys’┐Štem has been proposed. This web-based application provides EV users with an intelligent plat’┐Šform to discover, compare, and reserve charging stations efficiently. It integrates Multi-Attribute
Decision-Making (MADM) techniques and the TOPSIS (Technique for Order of Preference by
Similarity to Ideal Solution) algorithm to deliver personalized and optimized recommendations
based on user preferences such as waiting time, price, distance, and charging duration.
By combining real-time data processing, smart booking, and conflict handling, Obturo-EV ensures
a seamless charging experience that minimizes waiting time, prevents double booking, and pro’┐Šmotes fairness through queue management and penalty enforcement. Ultimately, the system aims
to make EV charging more reliable, convenient, and user-centric, encouraging wider adoption of
electric mobility .
1.1 Problem Statement
The rapid growth of electric vehicles has not been matched by an equally efficient and intelligent
charging network. Existing EV charging platforms are limited to basic filters such as nearest loca’┐Štion or static cost, without considering dynamic user requirements or real-time station availability.
This often leads to long waiting times, inefficient resource utilization, and double booking
conflicts. Moreover, the absence of unified data from multiple charging operators results in poor

LITERATURE SURVEY
2.1 Integrated Route Planning Algorithm Based on Spot Price and
Classified Travel Objectives for EV Users [1]
This paper presents an integrated route planning algorithm that provides optimal routes and cor’┐Šresponding charging schemes for EV users with different travel objectives based on spot price
forecasting and traffic conditions, combining traditional path planning algorithms with optimiza’┐Štion techniques.
2.1.1 Methodology
The methodology classifies EV user travel objectives into three categories: in-situ charging (find’┐Šing nearby stations), close-range travel (requiring one charging station), and multi-site travel (re’┐Šquiring multiple stations). Each category is further subdivided based on parking requirements
and duration. A time-window electricity price forecasting model based on GRU (Gated Recurrent
Unit) neural network with Encoder-Decoder framework is established to generate pricing informa’┐Štion. The model performs two types of prediction: intraday prediction (360 minutes) for long-term
trends and time-window prediction (60 minutes with higher accuracy) that updates every 15 min’┐Šutes. The integrated route planning algorithm combines Simulated Annealing Algorithm (SAA)
with A* and Dijkstra algorithms, selecting appropriate methods based on travel objectives.

The proposed system, named Obturo-EV - Intelligent EV Charger Booking and Management Sys’┐Štem , is designed to address the critical challenges of limited and fragmented EV charging infras’┐Štructure by providing a unified, reliable, and highly personalized service. The primary objective

It achieves this by combining real-time discovery, guaran’┐Šteed reservation management, and its core intelligence: the TOPSIS algorithm (Technique for Or’┐Šder Preference by Similarity to Ideal Solution). all available commercial and Peer-to-Peer (P2P) stations from best-fit to worst-fit. By integrat’┐Šing intelligent recommendation with robust Conflict Handling for slot booking, Obturo-EV aims
to revolutionize the EV charging experience by significantly minimizing user waiting time and
maximizing resource utilization.
